# The Zodiac Signs

## Aries (March 21 – April 19) — Fire, Cardinal, Mars-ruled
Aries is the initiator. Bold, direct, energetic, and often impatient. Planets in Aries act fast and want results now. The Sun in Aries creates someone who is self-motivated and needs to lead. The Moon in Aries processes emotions quickly and can be impulsive in emotional responses. Mars in Aries is at home — this person goes after what they want without hesitation.

## Taurus (April 20 – May 20) — Earth, Fixed, Venus-ruled
Taurus values stability, pleasure, and the physical world. Planets in Taurus operate slowly, deliberately, and with enormous staying power. Sun in Taurus creates someone grounded and determined, sometimes stubborn. Moon in Taurus needs comfort, routine, and sensory pleasure to feel secure. Venus in Taurus loves loyalty and physical affection.

## Gemini (May 21 – June 20) — Air, Mutable, Mercury-ruled
Gemini is curious, adaptable, and communicative. Planets in Gemini are quick, versatile, and interested in everything. Sun in Gemini creates a person who is witty, social, and mentally agile but can scatter their energy. Moon in Gemini processes feelings through talking and thinking rather than sitting with them.

## Cancer (June 21 – July 22) — Water, Cardinal, Moon-ruled
Cancer is the sign of home, family, and emotional memory. Planets in Cancer are sensitive, protective, and deeply connected to the past. Sun in Cancer creates someone who is nurturing and deeply feeling, with strong ties to family. Moon in Cancer is the Moon's natural home — this person feels everything deeply and needs emotional security. The 4th house is naturally associated with Cancer.

## Leo (July 23 – August 22) — Fire, Fixed, Sun-ruled
Leo wants to shine. It's the sign of creativity, self-expression, drama, and generosity. Planets in Leo are expressive and need an audience — or at least recognition. Sun in Leo creates someone with natural charisma who needs to be seen and appreciated. Moon in Leo needs to feel special and can be hurt by being overlooked.

## Virgo (August 23 – September 22) — Earth, Mutable, Mercury-ruled
Virgo is analytical, practical, and service-oriented. Planets in Virgo work carefully and seek to improve. Sun in Virgo creates someone who is detail-oriented, helpful, and often hard on themselves. Moon in Virgo tends to process feelings by analyzing them — worry is often the emotional expression here.

## Libra (September 23 – October 22) — Air, Cardinal, Venus-ruled
Libra seeks balance, beauty, and partnership. Planets in Libra are diplomatic and relationship-focused. Sun in Libra needs to relate to others to fully know themselves — they thrive in partnership. Moon in Libra needs harmony and can go to great lengths to avoid conflict.

## Scorpio (October 23 – November 21) — Water, Fixed, Pluto/Mars-ruled
Scorpio goes deep. It rules transformation, power, sexuality, and the unconscious. Planets in Scorpio are intense and don't do anything halfway. Sun in Scorpio creates someone with enormous depth, strong will, and often a private interior life. Moon in Scorpio feels with great intensity and needs deep emotional intimacy to feel safe.

## Sagittarius (November 22 – December 21) — Fire, Mutable, Jupiter-ruled
Sagittarius seeks meaning, truth, and freedom. Planets in Sagittarius are optimistic and expansive. Sun in Sagittarius creates someone philosophical, adventurous, and freedom-loving. Moon in Sagittarius needs space to roam — emotionally and physically. Hard to pin down, needs room to grow.

## Capricorn (December 22 – January 19) — Earth, Cardinal, Saturn-ruled
Capricorn is the climber — ambitious, disciplined, and responsible. Planets in Capricorn work for long-term results and don't expect shortcuts. Sun in Capricorn creates someone with strong drive to achieve and build something lasting. Moon in Capricorn can be emotionally reserved — they manage feelings rather than display them.

## Aquarius (January 20 – February 18) — Air, Fixed, Uranus/Saturn-ruled
Aquarius is the innovator and the humanitarian. Planets in Aquarius think differently and resist conformity. Sun in Aquarius creates someone who values independence, community, and progressive ideas. Moon in Aquarius processes feelings intellectually and can seem detached emotionally.

## Pisces (February 19 – March 20) — Water, Mutable, Neptune/Jupiter-ruled
Pisces is the mystic and the dreamer. Planets in Pisces are fluid, empathic, and imaginative. Sun in Pisces creates someone with deep creative sensitivity and often strong spiritual or artistic gifts. Moon in Pisces feels everything — the joys and sorrows of the whole world can wash through them.
